Key Challenges

Where the system breaks down

Severe Congestion

Roads operate at 150% capacity during peak hours. The average speed has dropped to 12 km/h in downtown areas.

Rising Pollution

Idling vehicles contribute to 60% of urban air pollution, leading to severe health risks for residents.

Last-Mile Gap

Metro stations are efficient, but getting from the station to the final destination remains difficult and expensive.

Information Blackout

Commuters lack real-time data. "When is the next bus?" is a guessing game, causing anxiety and delays.

Inefficient Parking

30% of traffic is just people looking for parking spots. Current lots are manually managed and inefficient.
